# Strategy

Practical guides for learning efficiently, managing complexity, and thinking clearly in technical environments.

| # | Document | Focus |
|---|----------|-------|
| 1 | [Shared Approach](01_shared_approach.md) | Structured way to learn new topics and make better decisions |
| 2 | [Shared Complexity](02_shared_complexity.md) | How complexity grows at scale and practical strategies to manage it |
| 3 | [Shared Critical Thinking](03_shared_critical_thinking.md) | Mental models, cognitive biases, trade-off analysis, and decision-making |
| 4 | [Integrated Reference](04_shared_systems_thinking.md) | Complete integration: lifecycle, performance stack, 14 principles, 8 patterns, dual decision engine |
| 5 | [Quantitative Reasoning](05_shared_quantitative_reasoning.md) | Statistical foundations + methods: why systems behave as they do, how to apply (estimation, Bayesian, flow economics, decision engine, applied scenarios) |
| 6 | [Quantitative Reference](06_shared_quantitative_reference.md) | Quick "what to do" guidance: rules, frameworks, situation→action. For TPMs, PMs, SWEs, DMs who aren't quants |

**Flow**: 01→02→03 build foundations. **04** integrates them into one systems reference—start here if you want the single consolidated view. **05** adds quantitative depth (why + how). **06** is the quick reference when you need the right move fast.

**Practical use**: Read 04 first for the big picture. Use 06 for daily decisions (rules, patterns, early warning signs). Go to 05 when you need to understand the reasoning or apply the full methods.
