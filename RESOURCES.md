## Notes

- **Standards Updates**: Standards and frameworks are regularly updated. Check official sources for latest versions.
- **Regional Variations**: Compliance requirements vary by region (GDPR for EU, CCPA for California, etc.). Ensure you understand applicable regulations.
- **Certification Requirements**: Many certifications require formal training and exams. Check official certification bodies for requirements.
- **Assessment Frequency**: Regular assessments (annual, quarterly) are recommended for security, compliance, and risk management.
